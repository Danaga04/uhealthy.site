Sacred Phrases from Solomon's Temple in Jerusalem

About the Declarations

These powerful declarations were extracted from the walls of Solomon's Temple in Jerusalem, originally written in ancient Hebrew. For centuries, Jewish merchants have used these phrases to:

  • Attract customers and opportunities
  • Close important deals
  • Multiply wealth and prosperity
  • Bless enterprises
Declaration 1
בָּרוּךְ אַתָּה יְיָ מַצְמִיחַ קֶרֶן יְשׁוּעָה
Pronunciation: Baruch Atah Adonai Matzmíach Keren Yeshuá
"Blessed are You, Lord, who makes the power of salvation flourish"
Usage: Recite before starting new businesses or ventures. This declaration invokes divine strength to make your projects prosper from the root.
Declaration 2
פּוֹתֵחַ אֶת יָדֶךָ וּמַשְׂבִּיעַ לְכָל חַי רָצוֹן
Pronunciation: Potêach Et Yadêcha Umasbia Lechol Chai Ratzon
"You open Your hand and satisfy the desire of every living creature"
Usage: To attract customers and abundance to your business. Recite when opening your establishment's doors in the morning.
Declaration 3
יְהוָה יִתֶּן עֹז לְעַמּוֹ
Pronunciation: Adonai Yiten Oz Le'amo
"The Lord will give strength to His people"
Usage: Before important meetings and difficult negotiations. This phrase grants spiritual strength and authority.
Declaration 4
וַיְבָרֶךְ אֹתְךָ יְהוָה אֱלֹהֶיךָ
Pronunciation: Vayevarech Otcha Adonai Elohêcha
"And the Lord your God will bless you"
Usage: To invoke blessings upon investments and financial applications. Recite when making important transactions.
Declaration 5
הָעֹשֶׁר וְהַכָּבוֹד מִלְּפָנֶיךָ
Pronunciation: Ha'osher Vehaacavod Milfanêcha
"Wealth and honor come from You"
Usage: Before job interviews and promotion opportunities. Attracts recognition and professional appreciation.
Declaration 6
מַרְבֶּה בְרָכָה וְהַצְלָחָה
Pronunciation: Marbê Berachá Vehatzlachá
"Multiplies blessing and prosperity"
Usage: To multiply results and profits. Use when closing contracts and business agreements.
Declaration 7
כִּי לְךָ הַגְּדֻלָּה וְהַגְּבוּרָה
Pronunciation: Ki Lechá Hagedulá Vehagvurá
"For Yours is the greatness and the power"
Usage: To conquer positions of leadership and authority. Recite before presentations and important speeches.
Declaration 8
נוֹתֵן לֶחֶם לְכָל בָּשָׂר
Pronunciation: Noten Lechem Lechol Basar
"Who gives food to all flesh"
Usage: To ensure constant and abundant provision. Ideal for merchants and entrepreneurs seeking continuous customer flow.
Declaration 9
תְּבָרֵךְ אֶת בֵּית עַבְדֶּךָ
Pronunciation: Tevarech Et Beit Avdêcha
"Bless the house of Your servant"
Usage: To bless your commercial establishment, office, or workplace. Recite when entering the environment.
Declaration 10
וְיִתֶּן לְךָ הָאֱלֹהִים מִטַּל הַשָּׁמַיִם
Pronunciation: Veyiten Lechá HaElohim Mital Hashamáyim
"And may God give you the dew of heaven"
Usage: To attract unexpected resources and opportunities from Above. Use when seeking new investors or partnerships.
Declaration 11
הַשְׁפַּע וְהַרְוָחָה יָבוֹא
Pronunciation: Hashpá Vehavachá Yavó
"Abundance and relief will come"
Usage: In times of financial difficulty. This declaration opens paths and attracts unexpected solutions.
Declaration 12
יְבָרֶכְךָ יְהוָה וְיִשְׁמְרֶךָ
Pronunciation: Yevarechechá Adonai Veyishmerécha
"The Lord bless you and keep you"
Usage: The most powerful of all. Use for complete protection and blessing over all your businesses and ventures. The ultimate priestly blessing.
